A guide to the spirit charmers deck

Some cards that you'll be using

Deck profile

The charmers

The charmers are the main focus of this deck (it is the spirit charmers deck after all). Their flip ability allows them to steal opponent's cards that are of the same type as them (water charmer would steal a water type monster when flipped). There are 6 charmers in total (one for each attribute)

Familiar-possessed

A stronger version of your charmers. They are mainly used to attack your opponent since they have significantly more attack power than their counterparts. When awakening of the possessed is on the field, they become a big threat to your opponent

The familiars

The familiars of the charmers. They usually have the highest attack damage in the deck and can be special summoned into the field by tributing their respective charmers along with any other card. When they are special summnoned, their abilities activate. These abilities are something between damage your opponent and bring back a charmer from the grave

Awakening of the possessed

The spell card that transforms your monsters from average cards to a force that will scare your opponent. This card increases your monster's attack by 300 for each unique attribute on your side of the field along with the added benefits of drawing a card every time you summon a monster and your cards being indestructible by spells and card effects

Unpossessed

The card that will raise your opponent's blood pressure. Basically makes your charmer monsters indestructible by battle, therefore the only way to remove them is by spell or card effects which can be negated by having awakening of the possessed on the field.

Spirit charmers

The deck's searcher card. By discarding one card, you can get 2 cards, one to your hand and the other set down on the field. The main use of this card is to get awakening of the possessed and unpossessed on the field.
